<p><strong>The Tunnel Boring Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Soft Ground TBMs</li><li>Hard Rock TBMs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tunnel Boring Machines (TBMs) are specialized equipment designed for underground construction, primarily used for creating tunnels in varying geological conditions. Soft Ground TBMs are suited for environments like clay and sand, utilizing mechanisms such as slurry or earth pressure balance to manage the surrounding soil. In contrast, Hard Rock TBMs are engineered to excavate through solid rock, employing heavy-duty rotary cutting heads to break the rock efficiently. These market types serve distinct applications, influencing project feasibility and alignment choices.</p></p>
