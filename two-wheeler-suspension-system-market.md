<p><p>The two-wheeler suspension system market includes several types designed to enhance ride quality and stability. Telescopic suspension features fork tubes that slide in and out, providing effective damping and support. Upside-down telescopic suspension improves rigidity and handling by reversing the fork design. Spring-loaded hydraulic suspension utilizes springs and hydraulic fluid for better shock absorption, ideal for rough terrains. Other systems may incorporate advanced technologies like air suspension or adjustable components, catering to diverse riding preferences and performance needs.</p></p>
