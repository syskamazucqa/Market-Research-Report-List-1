<p><strong>The Automotive Tempered Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full Tempered Glass</li><li>Zone Tempered Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive tempered glass market primarily features two types: full tempered glass and zone tempered glass. Full tempered glass is uniformly heated and then rapidly cooled, enhancing its strength and safety, making it ideal for windscreens and side windows. Zone tempered glass, on the other hand, undergoes selective heating, allowing for specific areas to be strengthened while preserving flexibility in other regions. This targeted approach enables tailored applications within vehicle designs, catering to varying structural and aesthetic needs.</p></p>
