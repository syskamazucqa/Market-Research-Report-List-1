<p><strong>The Three-way Control Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mixing Valves</li><li>Diverting Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The three-way control valves market primarily consists of mixing valves and diverting valves. Mixing valves combine two different fluid streams to achieve a desired temperature or composition, which is crucial in processes requiring precise control over mixed outputs. In contrast, diverting valves direct a single stream of fluid to multiple outlets, facilitating resource allocation in various applications. Both valve types are essential in industries like chemical processing, HVAC, and oil and gas, ensuring optimal system performance and efficiency.</p></p>
