<p><strong>The Epitaxial Silicon Wafer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polished wafer</li><li>Epitaxial wafer</li><li>Silicon-On-Insulator</li><li>Lapping wafer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The epitaxial silicon wafer market encompasses various types, each serving distinct applications. Polished wafers feature a smooth surface for basic semiconductor manufacturing. Epitaxial wafers allow for deposited silicon layers to enhance electronic properties. Silicon-on-insulator (SOI) wafers consist of a thin silicon layer atop an insulator, enabling reduced parasitic capacitance and improved performance in integrated circuits. Lapping wafers, engineered through mechanical polishing to achieve specific thicknesses, primarily cater to specialized applications where precise dimensional control is essential.</p></p>
