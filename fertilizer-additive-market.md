<p><p>The fertilizer additive market includes several types of functional agents that enhance fertilizer performance. Anticaking agents prevent clumping, ensuring smooth flow and application. Dedusting agents reduce dust generation during handling, improving safety and efficiency. Antifoam agents control foam in liquid fertilizers, facilitating better mixing processes. Hydrophobic agents repel water, maintaining product integrity in humid conditions. Corrosion inhibitors protect storage and application equipment from damage. Together, these additives optimize fertilizer usability, enhancing agricultural productivity and efficiency.</p></p>
