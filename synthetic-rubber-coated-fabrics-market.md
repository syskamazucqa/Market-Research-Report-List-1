<p><p>The synthetic rubber coated fabrics market comprises various types, each offering unique properties for diverse applications. Neoprene is valued for its durability and resistance to weather and chemicals, making it suitable for outdoor uses. Silicone rubber offers excellent heat resistance and flexibility. Butyl rubber provides airtightness and is ideal for medical applications. Nitrile rubber is known for oil resistance, commonly used in automotive and industrial settings. EPDM rubber is popular in roofing and automotive seals due to its excellent aging properties. Other specialty rubbers cater to niche demands.</p></p>
