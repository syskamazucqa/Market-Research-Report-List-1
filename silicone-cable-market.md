Silicone multicore cables consist of multiple conductors, providing flexibility and durability for diverse applications, including industrial and automotive environments. Both types benefit from silicone's excellent heat resistance, flexibility, and longevity, making them suitable for high-performance applications in extreme conditions.</p></p>
