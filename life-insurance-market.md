<p><p>The life insurance market primarily consists of two types: term insurance and permanent insurance. Term insurance offers coverage for a specific period, typically at lower premiums, making it suitable for those needing temporary protection. In contrast, permanent insurance provides lifetime coverage and includes cash value accumulation, often at higher premiums. This makes it ideal for individuals seeking long-term financial planning and stability. Both types cater to diverse financial needs and risk management strategies for policyholders.</p></p>
