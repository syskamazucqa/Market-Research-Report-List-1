<p><p>Practice Management Systems (PMS) are categorized into three main types: web-based, cloud-based, and on-premise. Web-based systems are accessible through browsers, offering flexibility and easy updates without installation. Cloud-based solutions store data on remote servers, enabling seamless access and collaboration from any location, with subscription models often in place. On-premise systems require local installation and management, providing greater control over data security but needing more resources for maintenance and updates. Each type caters to different operational needs and preferences.</p></p>
