<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Microneedle Array Patch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid Microneedles</li><li>Hollow Microneedles</li><li>Coated Microneedles</li><li>Dissolvable/Dissolving Microneedles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microneedle array patch market comprises four main types: </p><p>1. **Solid Microneedles** - These are used primarily for transdermal drug delivery, creating microchannels in the skin without delivering the medication directly. </p><p>2. **Hollow Microneedles** - Designed to deliver liquid formulations, these needles allow for the direct injection of drugs or vaccines into the dermal layers. </p><p>3. **Coated Microneedles** - These feature a drug coating that dissolves upon insertion, releasing medication as they penetrate the skin. </p><p>4. **Dissolvable Microneedles** - Made from biocompatible materials, they dissolve completely after insertion, delivering medication without leaving any residual materials.</p></p>
