Infrared tower fan heaters work by emitting infrared radiation, directly warming objects and people rather than the air, ensuring energy-efficient heating. The "others" category includes various unconventional designs and technologies, such as oil-filled or convection-based heaters, catering to diverse consumer needs and preferences in heating solutions.</p></p>
