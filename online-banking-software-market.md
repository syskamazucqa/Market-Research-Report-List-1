<p><ul><li>Cloud-based</li><li>On-premises</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The online banking software market comprises two primary types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based banking software is hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, offering scalability, lower upfront costs, and ease of updates. In contrast, on-premises banking software is installed locally on a bank's servers, providing greater control over data security and customization but requiring higher initial investment and maintenance. Both types cater to different banking needs and regulatory requirements, influencing their adoption across institutions.</p></p>
