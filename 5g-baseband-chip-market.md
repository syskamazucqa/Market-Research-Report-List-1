<p><p>The 5G Baseband Chip market is categorized into two main types: Single-Mode and Multi-Mode chips. Single-Mode 5G chips are designed to support only one specific 5G network type, making them ideal for devices focused on a single connectivity standard. In contrast, Multi-Mode 5G chips can operate across multiple network standards, providing greater flexibility and compatibility for devices requiring seamless connectivity across various networks. This versatility makes Multi-Mode chips increasingly popular in devices like smartphones and IoT applications.</p></p>
