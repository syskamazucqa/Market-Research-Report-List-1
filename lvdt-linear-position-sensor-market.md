<p><p>The LVDT (Linear Variable Differential Transformer) linear position sensor market encompasses two primary types: non-contact and contact position sensors. Non-contact position sensors operate without physical interaction with the object, offering higher durability and reduced wear, making them ideal for applications requiring high precision in harsh environments. Conversely, contact position sensors interact directly with the target, providing reliable feedback in applications where physical engagement is necessary. Both types play vital roles in automation, robotics, and industrial measurement sectors.</p></p>
