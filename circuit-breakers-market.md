<p><p>Circuit breakers are essential components for protecting electrical systems from overload and faults. The air circuit breaker uses air as an insulating medium, while the oil circuit breaker employs oil for insulation and arc extinction. Sulfur hexafluoride circuit breakers utilize SF6 gas for high-voltage applications, providing superior insulation. Vacuum circuit breakers operate in a vacuum to extinguish arcs, ensuring reliable performance. Other circuit breakers include mini, digital, and smart variants, catering to diverse industrial and commercial needs.</p></p>
