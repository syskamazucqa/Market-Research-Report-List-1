<p><p>The Olea Europaea oil market comprises three main types: Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Olive Oil, and Olive Pomace Oil. Extra Virgin Olive Oil is the highest quality, characterized by its superior taste and low acidity, made from the first cold pressing of olives. Olive Oil is a refined blend, offering a milder flavor and less acidity, suitable for cooking. Olive Pomace Oil, derived from the residue after extracting virgin oil, is the most economical option, often used for frying and in food processing.</p></p>
