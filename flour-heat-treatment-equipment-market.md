<p><p>Flour heat treatment equipment is essential for enhancing flour quality and safety. The dry heat treatment method involves applying high temperatures to flour without moisture, effectively reducing microbial load and improving shelf life. This method preserves the flour's natural properties while ensuring safety. In contrast, the wet heat treatment utilizes steam or hot water, which also sanitizes the flour but may alter its characteristics. Both methods serve specific applications, catering to different needs within the flour processing industry.</p></p>
