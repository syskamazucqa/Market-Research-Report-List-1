Wave energy generation captures energy from surface waves to create electricity. Ocean temperature difference systems exploit the thermal gradient between warm surface water and cold deep water, generating energy via thermodynamic cycles. Ocean current energy generation harnesses the consistent flow of ocean currents for power. Together, these methods contribute to sustainable energy solutions from marine environments.</p></p>
