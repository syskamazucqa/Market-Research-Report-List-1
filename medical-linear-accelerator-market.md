<p><strong>The Medical Linear Accelerator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low-Energy</li><li>High-Energy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Linear Accelerator (LINAC) market is categorized into low-energy and high-energy segments. Low-energy LINACs, typically in the range of 4 to 6 MeV, are primarily used for superficial tumors and skin treatments due to their limited penetration. In contrast, high-energy LINACs, operating at 6 MeV and above, are designed for deep-seated tumors, offering greater penetration and precision for advanced treatments like intensity-modulated radiation therapy (IMRT) and stereotactic body radiation therapy (SBRT). Both types are critical for effective cancer management.</p></p>
