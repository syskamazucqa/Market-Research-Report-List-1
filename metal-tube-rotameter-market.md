<p><p>A metal tube rotameter is a flow measurement device consisting of a tapered tube and a float that rises and falls within the tube to indicate the flow rate of liquids or gases. Renowned for their durability and accuracy, metal tube rotameters are predominantly used in various industrial applications, including oil and gas, water treatment, and chemical processing.</p><p>The Metal Tube Rotameter Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.3% during the forecast period.
