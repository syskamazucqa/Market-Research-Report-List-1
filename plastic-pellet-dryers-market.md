<p><p>The plastic pellet dryers market is divided into two main types: batch and continuous. Batch dryers operate by processing a specific quantity of pellets at a time, allowing for flexibility in managing various materials and moisture levels. In contrast, continuous dryers provide a steady flow of pellets through the drying process, enhancing efficiency and productivity for high-volume production. Each type caters to different operational needs, balancing between flexibility and high throughput based on the application's demands.</p></p>
