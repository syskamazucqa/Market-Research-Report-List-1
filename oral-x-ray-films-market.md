<p><p>The Oral X-ray Films market consists of three main types: Green, Half Speed Blue, and Full Speed Blue. Green films are sensitive to green light and require less radiation exposure, making them a safer choice. Half Speed Blue films provide a balance between speed and quality, suitable for general use. Full Speed Blue films are the fastest type, needing minimal radiation while delivering high-quality images; however, they may compromise some detail. Each type serves specific clinical needs based on exposure and image quality requirements.</p></p>
