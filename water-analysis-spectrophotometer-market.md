<p><ul><li>Single Beam</li><li>Double Beam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water analysis spectrophotometer market is primarily divided into single beam and double beam types. Single beam spectrophotometers measure light absorption through a single path, utilizing a reference measurement to ensure accuracy. In contrast, double beam spectrophotometers split the light path, allowing simultaneous measurement of the sample and a reference, resulting in enhanced precision and stability. Both types are essential for various water quality assessments, including detecting contaminants and verifying compliance with environmental regulations.</p></p>
