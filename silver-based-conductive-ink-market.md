<p><p>The silver-based conductive ink market primarily consists of three types: silver nanoparticles, silver flakes, and silver nanowires. Silver nanoparticles are tiny particles known for their high surface area and excellent conductivity, making them ideal for fine printed electronics. Silver flakes provide a balance of conductivity and flexibility, often used in applications requiring thicker layers. Silver nanowires offer superior conductivity and transparency, making them suitable for advanced applications like touchscreens and flexible displays. Each type serves distinct applications based on performance needs.</p></p>
