<p><ul><li>Semi-automatic CTP</li><li>Fully automatic CTP</li><li>Manual CTP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal Computer-to-Plate (CTP) market encompasses three main types: semi-automatic CTP, fully automatic CTP, and manual CTP. Semi-automatic CTP systems require some operator intervention for plate handling, balancing efficiency and control. Fully automatic CTP systems streamline the process with minimal human involvement, enhancing productivity and consistency. Manual CTP systems rely heavily on operator skills for plate processing, offering lower initial costs but higher labor demands. Each type caters to different production scales and operational preferences in the printing industry.</p></p>
