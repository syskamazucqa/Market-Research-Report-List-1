<p><strong>The Decorative Laminates Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High-pressure Laminate</li><li>Low-pressure Laminate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Decorative Laminates market is divided into two primary types: High-pressure Laminates (HPL) and Low-pressure Laminates (LPL). HPL is produced under high heat and pressure, resulting in a durable, resilient surface ideal for heavy-use applications like countertops and furniture. In contrast, LPL is made at lower pressures and is often used for lower-stress applications such as cabinetry and wall panels. Both types serve aesthetic and functional purposes, enhancing design while offering resistance to scratches and moisture.</p></p>
